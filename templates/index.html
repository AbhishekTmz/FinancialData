Bitcoin Network Analysis

Trade Activity

This graph shows how the trade volume (the total value of Bitcoin traded) compares to the transaction volume ratio (the number of Bitcoin transactions relative to total supply).

  1. A high trade volume indicates strong market activity, showing how much Bitcoin is being traded daily.
  2. A high transaction volume ratio suggests that more Bitcoin is being used in transactions rather than just being held.
  3. If trade volume is high but transactions are low, people might be investing in Bitcoin rather than using it for purchases. If transactions are high, Bitcoin is being used more like a currency.

Network Security
